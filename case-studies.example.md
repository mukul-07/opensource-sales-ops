## Example (fictional, illustrative only)

## Acme Logistics

**Industry:** Logistics tech
**Size:** 80 employees, Series A
**Use case:** SOC 2 Type I in 8 weeks ahead of enterprise deal

### The problem
Acme had a $900k enterprise deal blocked on SOC 2 readiness. Their existing auditor quoted 6 months and $80k.

### The solution
We ran the evidence collection and policy gap analysis in parallel, cutting scoping from 3 weeks to 4 days.

### Results
- SOC 2 Type I achieved in 8 weeks (vs 6 months quoted)
- $55k saved vs traditional auditor path
- $900k enterprise deal closed 4 months earlier

### Quote
> "We'd still be collecting screenshots without them. The deal wouldn't have closed this year."
> — Jane Doe, CTO, Acme Logistics

industry:logistics-tech, stage:series-a, use-case:soc2, persona:cto, trigger:enterprise-deal-blocker
